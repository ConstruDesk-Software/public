# Account Deletion Requirement – User Account Deletion Flow

This document describes how users can permanently delete their account **directly within the application**, in compliance with the **Google Play Account Deletion Requirement**.

---

## Account Deletion Availability

- Users can delete their account **after logging in** to the application.
- The account deletion option is available **inside the app**, without requiring external links, emails, or customer support requests.

---

## Steps to Delete the Account

### 1. Sign In

- The user signs in using their registered email and password.
- After successful authentication, the main application screen is displayed.

---

### 2. Open Account Settings

- From the bottom navigation bar, the user taps the **Profile / Account** icon.
- This opens the **Account & Settings** screen.

---

### 3. Select “Delete Account”

- The user scrolls to the bottom of the **Account & Settings** screen.
- A clearly visible **red button** labeled **“Delete Account”** is displayed.
- This option is always accessible to the user and not hidden behind support or external flows.

---

### 4. Confirm Irreversible Action

- When selecting **Delete Account**, a confirmation dialog titled **“Confirm Action”** is shown.
- The dialog clearly informs the user that:
  - The action is **permanent and irreversible**
  - **All user data will be permanently deleted**

---

- The **Delete Account** button remains disabled until the exact text is entered.

---

### 6. Final Confirmation and Deletion

- After confirmation:
  - The account is permanently deleted
  - All associated user data is removed from the system
- The user is logged out automatically after the deletion process.

---

## Data Deletion Policy

- All personal data associated with the user account is permanently deleted upon account deletion.
- No personal data is retained after deletion, except where legally required.
- The deletion process is immediate and does not require additional verification outside the application.

---

## Compliance Statement

This account deletion flow complies with the **Google Play Account Deletion Requirement**, ensuring that users can:

- Delete their account easily
- Perform the deletion directly inside the app
- Clearly understand the irreversible nature of the action
- Permanently remove their personal data without contacting support
